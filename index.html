How we met

Jerome and Glai’s story began as a simple introduction, two friends of a friend who believed it might be just another talking stage. But after a few chat messages, something clicked. Conversations flowed effortlessly, and before long, their first date arrived—a date where sparks began to fly, and both knew they wanted more. What started as casual exchanges soon grew into something undeniable, and Jerome quickly became an answered prayer for Glai. As they spent more time together and truly got to know one another, their bond deepened, and they fell even more in love, realizing that this connection was something extraordinary.

One year later, back in the very spot where they shared their first date, Jerome proposed, and Glai gave the sweetest "Yes." Their story proved that love doesn’t require a long journey to unfold—you simply need to find the right person. For Glai and Jerome, finding each other was all it took to make forever feel just right.
